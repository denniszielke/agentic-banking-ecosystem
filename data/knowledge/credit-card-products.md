# 3. Agent Guidance

## 3.1. Customer spends less than €1,000 per month

**3.1.1** Recommend: **ClassicCard**

### 3.1.2. Reasoning
- 3.1.2.1 Lowest annual fee
- 3.1.2.2 Sufficient credit limit
- 3.1.2.3 Good for online shopping and everyday purchases

### 3.1.3. Agent Statement
> **3.1.3.1** "Based on your spending habits, ClassicCard provides the essential features you need without paying for premium travel benefits."

---

## 3.2. Customer travels several times per year

**3.2.1** Recommend: **GoldCard**

### 3.2.2. Reasoning
- 3.2.2.1 Travel insurance included
- 3.2.2.2 Better international usage conditions
- 3.2.2.3 Higher credit limit
- 3.2.2.4 Travel cashback benefits

### 3.2.3. Agent Statement
> **3.2.3.1** "Because you travel regularly, GoldCard may provide additional value through travel insurance and lower international usage costs."

---

## 3.3. Customer travels internationally every month

**3.3.1** Recommend: **PlatinumCard**

### 3.3.2. Reasoning
- 3.3.2.1 No foreign currency fees
- 3.3.2.2 Airport lounge access
- 3.3.2.3 Comprehensive insurance package
- 3.3.2.4 Premium support services

### 3.3.3. Agent Statement
> **3.3.3.1** "PlatinumCard is designed for frequent international travellers and includes premium travel services that can offset the higher annual fee."
