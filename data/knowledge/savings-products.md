# 3. Agent Guidance

## 3.1. Customer Balance Below €5,000

**3.1.1** **Recommended Product:** FlexSave

### 3.1.2. Reasoning
- 3.1.2.1 No minimum balance requirement
- 3.1.2.2 Immediate access to funds
- 3.1.2.3 Suitable for emergency savings
- 3.1.2.4 No commitment period

### 3.1.3. Agent Statement
> **3.1.3.1** "Based on your balance and need for flexibility, FlexSave appears suitable because you can access your savings at any time while still earning interest."

---

## 3.2. Customer Balance Between €5,000 and €25,000

**3.2.1** **Recommended Products:** FlexSave and GrowthSaver

### 3.2.2. Decision Guidance

#### 3.2.2.1. Choose **FlexSave** if
- 3.2.2.1.1 Access to funds is important
- 3.2.2.1.2 Savings may be needed unexpectedly
- 3.2.2.1.3 Customer prioritises liquidity

#### 3.2.2.2. Choose **GrowthSaver** if
- 3.2.2.2.1 Funds are unlikely to be used in the next three months
- 3.2.2.2.2 Customer prefers a higher interest rate
- 3.2.2.2.3 Building medium-term savings reserves

### 3.2.3. Agent Statement
> **3.2.3.1** "You may benefit from either FlexSave or GrowthSaver. GrowthSaver provides a higher return but requires a 90-day notice period for withdrawals."

---

## 3.3. Customer Balance Above €25,000

**3.3.1** **Recommended Product:** FixedDeposit Plus

### 3.3.2. Decision Guidance
#### 3.3.2.1. Ask
**3.3.2.1.1** 1. How long can the funds remain invested?
**3.3.2.1.2** 2. Will access to the money be required during the investment period?
**3.3.2.1.3** 3. Is a guaranteed return more important than flexibility?

#### 3.3.2.2. Recommend FixedDeposit Plus when
- 3.3.2.2.1 Investment horizon exceeds one year
- 3.3.2.2.2 Customer wants predictable returns
- 3.3.2.2.3 Liquidity requirements are low

### 3.3.3. Agent Statement
> **3.3.3.1** "FixedDeposit Plus offers guaranteed returns for customers who do not need immediate access to their funds and are comfortable committing their savings for a fixed period."

# 6. Compliance Rules

## 6.1. The agent may

- 6.1.1 Explain product features and conditions
- 6.1.2 Compare available products
- 6.1.3 Estimate interest earnings based on customer input
- 6.1.4 Help customers understand trade-offs between flexibility and returns

### 6.1.5. The agent must not

- 6.1.5.1 Provide personalised financial advice
- 6.1.5.2 Guarantee future earnings
- 6.1.5.3 Recommend investment strategies without suitability assessment
- 6.1.5.4 Approve product applications or credit decisions

**6.1.5.5** If a conversation moves into investment advice or suitability assessment, the customer should be referred to a qualified financial advisor.
